About me

Hello, my name is Péter Laczó, and I was born in a small-sized city in Hungary called Békéscsaba. I live in a small-but-cosy flat with my girlfriend (called Lívia) in the very centre of Budapest.

I recently graduated from my Master's as an Info-Bionics Engineer, a specific interdisciplinary engineering profession combining computer, biological, and medical sciences. Nevertheless, I currently work as a Data Engineer at a medium-sized data company nearby our place.

Although we love our friends and family here, Lívia and I are aiming to have some international adventure by moving to another European country in the near future.
